## What is Artificial Intelligence ?
Advanced computer programs that can discover complex patterns and answer questions just like natural intelligence, decide, and generate content. Artificial Intelligence is not a magical mind, it is set of amazing algorithms and accuration calculations.

## Types of Artificial Intelligence
* **ANI (Artificial Narrow Intelligence)** : Systems that are designed only for specific purposes like voice assistants and chess algorithms. we are currently in this stage.
* **AGI (Artificial General Intelligence)** : A system which is same in understanding and intelligence as a human. This stage is theory right now.
* **ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence)** : Machines which are way more intelligent rather than humans in every aspect (e.g. creativity, problem solving). This stage is a projection of Unknown Future.

## Machine Learning
Learning automatically from experience and pattern rather than receiving commands. For instance, we provide 10000 images of apple and peach to the machine, and it will learn differences without any specified formula.
